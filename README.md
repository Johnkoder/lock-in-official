### What’s new

- Adds continuous 60-day seasons beginning on the profile's creation date.
- Applies rank resets at season rollover while preserving lifetime XP, statistics,
  unlocked portraits, and selected portraits.
- Shows season days remaining and rank progress from the new season's starting rank.
- Adds frozen season history with starting rank, finishing rank, earned XP, and reset destination.
- Reconciles elapsed seasons safely at local midnight and summarizes rollovers once.
